Shipping

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery costs in the territory of Serbia are 150 RSD for orders up to 2500 RSD. Over that amount, shipping is free. For orders from abroad, delivery costs are 1800 RSD for orders up to 10 books. For orders over 10 books delivery is 6800 RSD.

Returns

What is the procedure for claiming incorrectly delivered products?

The procedure for resolving complaints is carried out on the basis of the provisions of the Law on Consumer Protection (“Official Gazette of the RS” No. 62/2014) and the provisions of the Law on Trade (“Official Gazette of the RS” No. 53/10, 10/2013). You are entitled to a replacement of defective products if the product clearly shows that the defect was not caused by improper use, but that there was an error in production, packaging or transport. The deadline to expire malpractice is 2 years from the date of purchase. The deadline for responding to the complaint is 8 days from the receipt of the complaint. The deadline for resolution is 15 days from receipt of the complaint. In this case, the return of goods is made at our expense and the registration is in three simple steps:

  • 1. Send your request to our operator at the address of mailto:complaint@magicbook.comMake sure you provide the following information: name and surname, e-mail address, the invoice or cash invoice number provided with the goods and a message describing the reason for the complaint.
  • 2. Pack the items intended for return properly in order not to damage the transport. Be sure to attach an invoice or cash invoice, and write on the envelope "complaint" and the document number you received. Please do not forget to submit the original document (invoice or cash invoice), because without it, unfortunately, we are not able to replace the goods.
  • 3. After the damaged goods arrive at our premises and the claim is verified, the correct item will be delivered to your address. If the item is no longer available, a refund will be made.

Replacement of the damaged item is at the seller's expense and is done through the appropriate courier service. You cannot leave the goods personally at any of the MagicBook bookstores.
